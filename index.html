Lexi - YDS Words

Lexi - YDS Words is a mobile application designed specifically for Turkish speakers preparing for the YDS exam. The app serves as your personal English vocabulary assistant, helping you master essential words through interactive learning tools and smart study features.

Features

  • Interactive Flashcards: Study English-Turkish word pairs with interactive flashcards for efficient learning
  • Multiple Learning Modes: Quiz mode, word matching games, and personalized word lists
  • Smart Notifications: Customizable reminders to maintain your study schedule
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your improvement with detailed statistics
  • Offline Access: Study anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection
  • Personalized Lists: Create and manage your own word collections

Tech Stack

  • React Native & Expo: For building and deploying the mobile application
  • MMKV: For efficient and fast storage
  • Expo Notifications: For handling study reminders
  • React Navigation: For navigation within the app
  • React Native Reanimated: For smooth animations
  • Expo Application: For accessing application details

Target Audience

  • YDS Exam Candidates
  • Turkish Speakers Learning English
  • Self-Paced Learners
  • Busy Professionals
